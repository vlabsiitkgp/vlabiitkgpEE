Objective: Experimental verification of frequency response of R-L-C series Circuit.

Series RLC circuits consist of a resistance, a capacitance and an inductance connected in series across an alternating supply. Series RLC circuits are classed as second-order circuits because they contain two energy storage elements, an inductance L and a capacitance C. Consider the RLC circuit below. Procedure
  1. Connect the circuit elements as mentioned below
    1-3 ,4-5 ,8-6, 6-10, 9-11, 2-7, 7-9(ex:-Drag from 1 and click on 3.)
  2. 3 volt p-p sinusoidal voltage is applied at the input.Put R=100Ω ,L=0.1H, C=0.1uF.
  3. Switch on the oscilloscope by clicking "ON" button & click twice the Sine wave button for input signal & click on the"Output" button to observe the output.Channel & Channel2 button shows the input & output signals respectively. Dual, shows both of them.
  4. Change the Frequency of input signal by the Frequency knob from 100Hz to 5KHz & observe the corresponding output voltage signal.
  5. Switch off the oscilloscope

  6. Click on Tableshow button each time after clicking on "output" button to see observation each time.
  7. Click on 'GainPlot' button to see the frequency response plot(Gain(dB)vs.Frequency(Hz))& click on the 'Phaseplot'button to see the corresponding phase response.Click on Clear button to clear datas.
  8. Switch off the oscilloscope.Observe different results for different values of ζ (0.1,0.2,0.3,0.5,0.7).
  9. From the Gain plots the maximum gain,-3dB frequency can be observed for different ζ .
